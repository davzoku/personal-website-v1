This article is part of the [Draw with Walter Series](/draw-with-walter) and it covers the topic on making digital line art on Procreate.

Made famous by Pablo Picasso in the early 20th century, one line drawing is the technique of drawing realistic illustrations in the form of a single unbroken line.

## Tutorials

### One Line Drawing Tutorial by Alessa Jo on YouTube

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=38H0VMJD2Cs

- A straight-forward tutorial on making one line art by tracing over an image in Procreate.
- Brushes: Calligraphy - Monoline; Streamline 100 (max)
- I think it is a good way for practice and learn which features are essential to include in the line

### One Line Drawing Timelapse by With One Line on YouTube

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kv4WoDNDxpo

- Drawing one line drawing on Procreate freehand by WithOneLine
- Brushes: unknown; it has pressure sensitivity settings. It adds depth to the drawing.

### One Line Drawing Timelapse 2 by With One Line on YouTube

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j0VGb_E-msk

- Drawing one line drawing on Procreate with symmetry mode
