### Features:

<BasicImage
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/wtkw/image/upload/v1626364961/waltertengcom/projects/glimpse/example_rwmvhx.jpg"
  alt="Sites may or may not have the option to show password"

_Not all websites provide the option to toggle the visibility of the inputted passwords._

This may be frustrating when you think you have mistyped a letter.

Glimpse offers a unified experience across all websites to easily show/hide any password fields with shortcut keys **(ALT + P)** or via the context menu (right-click) whenever you need it.

The shortcut key is also configurable via the chrome extensions shortcut menu.
